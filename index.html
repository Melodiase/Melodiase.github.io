Maryana Smirnova

I’m Maryana, an AI explorer with a builder’s mindset. I studied Artificial Intelligence at Deggendorf Institute of Technology, where I focused on bringing AI into the real world, especially through robotics and data modeling. I’ve worked with practical tools across computer vision, classical geometry and data pipelines to solve problems that connect algorithms with the physical world.

At Bayer AG, I interned as a Data Scientist, working on predictive model for chemical formulations. I combined experimental lab data with machine learning to generate insights that actually helped improve outcomes. That experience showed me what AI can do when it's grounded in real context.

Since graduating, I’ve shifted my focus to a startup project that’s now my full-time mission. Welcome Interview Ninja— a real-time assistant that helps people hack job interviews as they happen. It’s built for smart, capable minds who get boxed out by outdated norms and performative hiring games. This isn’t prep — it’s leverage. A tool that quietly flips the script, giving clever people the edge they need to show their true value.

I believe AI should be practical, fast, and human-centered. It should break down barriers and create access. That’s what I’m building — and that’s what drives me every day.
